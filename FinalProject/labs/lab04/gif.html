This GIF is from giphy, a popular site to get GIFs from for many purposes, from humor to information. The GIF above is Michael, from the office, doing finger guns at the camera.

The file type of the image above is GIF, which stands for Graphics Interchange Format. This format is commonly used for animated images, as it is popular in social media and online.
